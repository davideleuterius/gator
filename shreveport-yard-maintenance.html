LAWN & GROUNDS SERVICES

Basic Lawn Services

Our basic lawn service will leave your grounds with a manicured look creating a neat appearance and real curb appeal. We will mow all grass areas, and weed eat/edge around yard features, driveways, and sidewalks. Blowing off concrete surfaces completes a finished appearance.

Gator Ground Works offers free on-site estimates for each location, as well as weekly and bi-weekly services to suit residential and commercial customers needs.

Gator Ground Works offers our lawn customers hedge trimming services for all appropriate border areas. We also bag leaves and pine straw. These materials make great compost. Pine straw is also a natural deterrent to insects. We can leave the bagged yard waste for your own yard use or, we can call haul it away.

Gator Ground Works can also clean off your rooftop, gutters and deck to remove any debris, including limbs, leaves, and pine straw. We can bag this waste as well.

For those customers with thick weeds and bullgrass bordering sidewalks and driveways, we offer a gas powered edging service with a machine tough enough to make short work of stubborn weeds.

Free on-site estimates are always available for additional lawn services. Gator Ground Works is committed to leaving a beautifully manicured lawn for our customers to enjoy.
