Junk & Debris Removal

Gator Ground Works offers junk removal services for residential and commercial customers. Many customers are aware that the city garbage service does not remove all items, nor does a special pick up occur in a timely manner.

We price junk removal by 1⁄4 trailer, 1⁄2 trailer and full trailer loads. We then pay the parish landfill to dispose of the trash. Some materials may be hazardous and will incur an additional charge.

Get Gator Ground Works to remove your yard / storm debris and junk to create more usable space, and enhance the appearance of your grounds.

Small Scale Demo Service & Removal

As time passes sheds, out buildings, decks, and fences become deteriorated and require removal for safety as well as appearance sake. Deer stands age after years of use. Play houses, tree houses, swing sets, and play equipment outlive their usefulness and grow shabby.

Gator Ground Works can demo these yard fixtures and haul them away to create more usable space to beautify your grounds. As always, we offer free on-site estimates for all our services.
