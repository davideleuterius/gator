Building & Growing community-based relationships

Gator Ground Works is a service company that can meet a variety of needs for commercial and residential customers. The Gator Ground Works family has deep roots and commitment to the Shreveport/Bossier community. Our goal at Gator Ground Works is to build and grow community-based relationships by doing business with, and for other locally owned and managed businesses. This will allow both us, and the community that we love, serve, and live in to grow together.

Lawn clean ups and weekly/bi-weekly maintenance plans are one of the many ways we keep your grounds beautiful. Other services we offer are junk/debris removal, and power washing service. Specifically for our commercial customers, we offer parking lot spot cleaning and re- striping of parking and safety lines. We can make a custom grounds package to include specific services tailored to your business needs, as a one time, or reccurring service.

Our owner, Brandon Burnham, does all estimates on-site at no cost. For direct communication, best results, and establishment of personal relationships, contact Gator Ground Works.
